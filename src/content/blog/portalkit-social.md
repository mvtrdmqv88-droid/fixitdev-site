## Product Hunt

**Tagline:** Stop chasing clients for files. One link, one portal, everything you need.

**Description:**

PortalKit is a client onboarding portal builder for freelancers and agencies. Instead of collecting project info and assets through messy email chains, you build a custom form (drag & drop, no code), share a single link, and your client submits everything in one place. No sign-up required for clients.

Key features:
- Drag & drop form builder
- Multiple field types (text, file upload, dropdown, date, checkbox)
- Shareable portal links
- No client account needed
- Mobile-friendly

Free tier includes 1 portal. Pro ($19/mo or $49 one-time) gives you unlimited portals.

Built by a freelancer who got tired of sending "reminder: please send your logo" emails.

**Maker Comment:**

Hey PH! I'm the solo dev behind PortalKit.

The story is pretty simple: I'm a freelance web designer, and every project starts the same way -- an awkward email chain trying to get the client's logo, brand colors, content, and credentials. It takes forever, things get lost, and I end up sending follow-up emails that make me feel like a debt collector.

So I built what I wished existed: a simple portal where I define exactly what I need, share one link, and the client fills it all out. No account for them, no complexity.

The free tier gives you one portal, which is genuinely enough to test the workflow. I use the Pro version myself because I have multiple active clients, but one portal covers the basics.

Built with React 18 + Vite + @dnd-kit for the drag and drop. Would love your feedback!
